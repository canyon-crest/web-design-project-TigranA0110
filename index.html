Childhood

I was born in Moscow, Russia, on April 1st, 2009. My ethnicity is Armenian. About a year after I was born, my parents had my younger brother. We lived in a studio in the city. After a few years, we moved to a house in a small community outside of the city. Most of my memories are from there, but I do remember the city. There was a little river next to our house in the forest through which fresh water ran. We would often go there to drink from it or to use the shower area, which was a small wooden box with a shower head connected to the river. There was also a set of train tracks where my brother and I would always go to sit and talk. During the winter, when it snowed, we would ride our bikes around the neighborhood, and our dad would make a big slide in our backyard while we were out riding. The neighborhood’s name is “новое ступино” or Novoye Stupino in English. I know 3 different languages, Russian, Armenian, and English.
